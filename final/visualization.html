The Growth of the Video Game Industry

Throughout the last few decades, the rise of technology evolution has tranformed gaming from simple arcade games in the 90's to complex AAA games in our modern world.
To better capture this, this visualisation aims to explore the impact of the video game industry as can be seen from the games sales, consoles released as well as the need to keep up with the era of technological enhancements.
